WHAT IS BMI

BMI is a widely used measure to assess an individual’s weight relative to their height. It provides a rough estimate of body fatness based on a person’s weight and height. The BMI calculation is a simple ratio of weight in kilograms (kg) divided by the square of height in meters (m^2)

How To Calculate Your BMI:   BMI = weight (kg) / height² (m²)
